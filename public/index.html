Find out more

Create and manage weekly IT project reports!

Create your weekly project report in a few minutes. Concentrate on the content instead of copying the same slides every week, just to update a few lines of texts. See how PReports lets you create your reports in a breeze.

Try the demo!

Features

Organized

Organize your reports on a weekly basis. Create a new report in seconds.

Search

Easily find the report you are looking for.

Fast

Edit on the go and let the auto save do the rest.

Share

Finished the report? Just send your colleaques the link or notify them via mail or slack.

Copy

New week, new report. Duplicate last weeks report with just two clicks.

Undo

Made a mistake? Undo your last 20 edits.

Security

Supports CAS out of the box. Plugin for other auth systems possible.

OpenSource

PReports is open source. Feel free to grab and modify it.
